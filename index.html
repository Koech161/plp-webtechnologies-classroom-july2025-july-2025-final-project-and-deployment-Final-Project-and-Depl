About Me

Capturing Stories Through My Lens

Hello! I'm Alex, a professional photographer with over 10 years of experience specializing in wedding, portrait, and commercial photography.

My approach combines technical expertise with a keen eye for emotion and storytelling. I believe that the best photographs are those that capture genuine moments and emotions.

Based in New York, I'm available for assignments worldwide. My work has been featured in several publications and exhibitions.
